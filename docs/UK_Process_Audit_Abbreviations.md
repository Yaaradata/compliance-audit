# UK Process Audit — Abbreviations & Glossary

A reference of every short form, acronym and initialism used across the **UK Process Audit** (route `/UK_Process_Audit/v2`) — its 8 domains, 95 controls, obligations, regulators, evidence types and source systems.

---

## 1. Regulators & authorities

| Abbreviation | Full form | Note |
|---|---|---|
| **FCA** | Financial Conduct Authority | Conduct regulator (BCOBS, CONC, DISP, Consumer Duty, SUP). |
| **PRA** | Prudential Regulation Authority | Prudential regulator (depositor protection, credit risk). |
| **BoE** | Bank of England | Operates RTGS/CHAPS settlement. |
| **HM Treasury** | Her Majesty's Treasury | Issuing body for MLR/sanctions legislation. |
| **NCA** | National Crime Agency | Receives SARs; financial-crime enforcement. |
| **OFSI** | Office of Financial Sanctions Implementation | Sanctions reporting & enforcement. |
| **PSR** | Payment Systems Regulator | APP scam reimbursement regime. |
| **ICO** | Information Commissioner's Office | UK data-protection regulator. |
| **FOS** | Financial Ombudsman Service | Independent complaint dispute resolution. |
| **SFO** | Serious Fraud Office | Bribery/fraud enforcement. |
| **Pay.UK** | Pay.UK (payment scheme operator) | Runs Bacs, FPS, CoP, CASS. |
| **UKEB** | UK Endorsement Board | Endorses IFRS for UK use. |
| **IASB** | International Accounting Standards Board | Issues IFRS standards. |
